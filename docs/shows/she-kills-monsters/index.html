She Kills Monsters

written by Qui Nguyen
directed by Brandon McCallister

Mourning the loss of her family, Agnes is packing up her childhood home, the only one she’s ever known—when she discovers her late sister’s Dungeons and Dragons module. The tables are turned. The dice come out. Her teenage sister’s private world is summoned in gameplay: together, they will befriend demons, decimate gelatinous cubes, and venture into forgotten, smoke-filled dragon lairs as Agnes fights to bring home her sister’s soul.
