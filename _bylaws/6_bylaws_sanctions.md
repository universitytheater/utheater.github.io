## VII. Bylaw Violation Sanctions

### A. Imposing Sanctions

1. As the governing body of UT, Committee is responsible for ensuring that the standards set by the Bylaws are met by all community members. As such, Committee may impose sanctions on productions or individuals who are in violation of the Bylaws.  
     
2. Sanctions may only be enacted with a two-thirds majority vote from Committee.  
     
3. Committee should make every effort to ascertain the details of the situation before calling a vote for sanctions in this way.

4. #### Sanctions Regarding Sections V & VI 
    In the case that an individual or production is in violation of the Bylaws, the sanctions imposed by Committee may include but are not limited to:  
    * Automatic rejection of a proposal.   
    * Reduction of funding from a production.   
    * Cancellation of a production.   
    * Removal of personnel from a production.   
    * Removal of personnel from specific duties on a production.   
    * Suspension of personnel from a production. 

5. #### Sanctions on Committee Members
    In the case that a member of Committee is in violation of any section of the Bylaws, the sanction imposed by Committee may include but are not limited to:  
    * Removal from Committee, pursuant to Section I.B.3.b  
    * Removal from an Officer position, pursuant to Section I.B.4.b
